About GED

General Educational Diploma (GED) is high school equivalency diploma, which is recognized and accepted by nearly all U.S. colleges, employers and universities. The test includes 4 subjects - Language Arts, Science, Mathematics and Social Studies. Students are not required to take all 4 test at once - you may choose to take the tests separately at your own pace.

GED Subjects

Language Art

Topics include:

  • Reading for Meaning
  • Identifying and Creating Arguments
  • Grammar and Language

Test format

  • 3 sections
  • 1 written essay
  • Multiple choice and other question types

Time allocation

  • 150 minutes
  • includes 45 minutes for the written essay
  • includes 10 minute break
