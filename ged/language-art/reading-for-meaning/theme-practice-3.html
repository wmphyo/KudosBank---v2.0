Theme Exercise 3

Question 1

In John Steinbeck's 'Of Mice and Men', the theme of friendship and loneliness is evident. The story revolves around two displaced ranch workers, George and Lennie, who form a deep bond as they pursue their dream of owning land. Despite the harsh realities of their lives, their friendship provides them with hope and a sense of belonging.

Question 2

In Charlotte Bronte's 'Jane Eyre', the theme of independence and self-respect is central. Jane's journey from a mistreated orphan to a strong, self-assured woman illustrates her determination to maintain her dignity and integrity, even in the face of societal pressures and personal hardship.

Question 3

In William Golding's 'Lord of the Flies', the theme of civilization versus savagery is prominent. The novel depicts a group of boys stranded on a deserted island who gradually abandon their civilized behavior and descend into barbarism, highlighting the thin veneer of society and the underlying darkness within human nature.

Question 4

In F. Scott Fitzgerald's 'Tender is the Night', the theme of the decline of the American Dream is explored through the lives of Dick and Nicole Diver. Their glamorous lifestyle and apparent success mask a deeper instability and moral decay, reflecting the disillusionment with the American Dream during the Jazz Age.

Question 5

In Alice Walker's 'The Color Purple', the theme of female empowerment and resilience is central. The protagonist, Celie, overcomes severe abuse and oppression to find her own voice and strength. The novel emphasizes the importance of sisterhood and self-discovery in the face of adversity.

1. Based on the passage, which of the following statements best represents the theme of the novel 'Of Mice and Men'?

2. Which of the following statements best represents the theme of the novel 'Jane Eyre'?

3. Which of the following statements best represents the theme of the novel 'Lord of the Flies'?

4. Which of the following statements best represents the theme of the novel 'Tender is the Night'?

5. Which of the following statements best represents the theme of the novel 'The Color Purple'?
