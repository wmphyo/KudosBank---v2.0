Summary Exercise 3

Question 1

The town of Oakville has two prominent community centers: The Oakville Sports Complex and The Oakville Arts Center. The Sports Complex offers facilities for various sports including swimming, basketball, and tennis, attracting athletes and fitness enthusiasts. The Arts Center, on the other hand, provides classes and workshops in painting, music, and theater, drawing artists and performers. Residents enjoy having both options, though there is a friendly rivalry over which center provides greater benefits to the community.

Question 2

Renewable energy sources such as solar and wind power have gained popularity as alternatives to fossil fuels. Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, and wind turbines generate power from the wind. These technologies offer environmental benefits by reducing greenhouse gas emissions and reliance on non-renewable resources. However, challenges such as energy storage and initial setup costs need to be addressed to fully realize their potential.

Question 3

In the small village of Greenfield, an annual Harvest Festival is held to celebrate the end of the farming season. The festival features traditional music, dancing, and local food. Farmers showcase their best produce, and visitors participate in various games and contests. The event fosters a sense of community and highlights the importance of agriculture in the region.

Question 4

The development of virtual reality (VR) technology has transformed various industries, including gaming, education, and healthcare. In gaming, VR provides immersive experiences that transport players into different worlds. In education, VR can simulate historical events and scientific phenomena, enhancing learning. In healthcare, VR is used for surgical training and therapy. Despite its advantages, VR also raises concerns about privacy and the potential for addiction.

Question 5

The Amazon rainforest, often referred to as the "lungs of the Earth," plays a crucial role in regulating the global climate. It absorbs large amounts of carbon dioxide and produces oxygen, supporting biodiversity and indigenous communities. However, deforestation due to logging, agriculture, and mining threatens the rainforest's health and the planet's ecological balance.

1. Which of the following statements best summarizes the difference in opinion over the community centers in Oakville?

2. Which of the following statements best summarizes the benefits and challenges of renewable energy?

3. Which of the following statements best summarizes the Harvest Festival in Greenfield?

4. Which of the following statements best summarizes the impact of virtual reality (VR) technology?

5. Which of the following statements best summarizes the importance of the Amazon rainforest?
