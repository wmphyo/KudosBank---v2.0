Exercise 3

1. Ashanti’s Bike Store makes a profit of 17.5% on sales. If this month’s profit is $2175, how much did the store sell this month? (Round to the nearest cent.)

2. The number of traffic accidents in our city has dropped by 17% this year. If last year’s total was 475, what is this year’s total?

3. If Kris makes d dollars and Heidi makes 75 dollars less than 3 times Kris’s wage, what does Heidi make in terms of d?

4. If Stephanie has 5c CDs for every DVD Alyshia has, and Jeff has 4d DVDs, which is 15 more than Alyshia has, how many CDs does Stephanie have?

5. The price of a carton of computer paper decreased from $25 to $20. What was the percent decrease in the price?
